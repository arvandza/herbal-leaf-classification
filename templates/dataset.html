"About Dataset"

There are ten plant species contained in this dataset, such as Averrhoa bilimbi (Blimbing Wuluh),Psidium guajava (Jambu Biji), Citrus Aurantiifolia (Jeruk Nipis) , Ocimum Africanum (Kemangi), Aloe vera (Lidah Buaya), Artocarpus heterophyllus (Nangka), Pandanus Amaryllifolius (Pandan), Carica papaya (Pepaya), Apium graveolens (Seledri), Piper Betle (Sirih). Labels are named according to names in Indonesia.
